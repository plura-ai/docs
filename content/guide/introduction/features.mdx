Plura's AI agents continuously learn and improve from each interaction, ensuring increasingly accurate and relevant responses over time.

- RESTful API

## Customization Options

Plura offers extensive customization to tailor the platform to your needs:

1. **Branding**: Customize the look and feel of your AI interactions to match your brand identity.
2. **Response Templates**: Create and manage templates for common responses or specific scenarios.
3. **Workflow Integration**: Design custom workflows to handle complex interaction scenarios.
4. **Fallback Mechanisms**: Set up human handover protocols for situations the AI can't handle.
